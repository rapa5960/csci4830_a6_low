This is low vision mode. From using the magnifier, I learned that it is easier to see depending on the contrast. In general, having a light text on a dark background makes it easier to see. I also learned that it is hard to scroll through pages because, due to the large font, it scrolls very quickly. The features I added to low vision mode are: 200% size text, white color text, black background throughout.
